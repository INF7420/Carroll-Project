About Me

As a household manager, childcare specialist, and full-time graduate student at Wayne State University, Melanie Carroll leads a chaotic lifestyle. Orignally born in Phildelphia, Pennsylvania, Melanie migrated to the Midwest, specifically Wichita, Kansas after her and her husband finished their respective enlistments in the U.S. Navy. Having travelled the Pacific and resided in different U.S. geographic regions, they decided to call the Midwest their home due to its slower paced lifestyle. The full-time caretaker to two children (seen pictured: Micky (4) and Evie(3)), Melanie decided to work towards her Master's in Library Science and Information for her return to the professional workforce in the coming years.
